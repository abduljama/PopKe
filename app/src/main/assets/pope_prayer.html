Almighty and ever living father,

You chose your servant Pope Francis as the successor of apostle Peter and Shepard of your flock.

Look favourably, we pray, on his pastoral visit to our nation Kenya. Inspire us to receive him well.

May your Holy Spirit enlighten our minds and hearts to be generous and receptive to his message and encouragement.

Grant that he may be for us, your people, a visible bond of unity which brings healing and reconciliation, love and peace;

so that in you, the Shepard of souls, all may know the truth and attain eternal life. Amen
